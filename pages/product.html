What TOG rating should I choose? ▾

TOG 2.5 is suitable for room temperatures of 16–20°C (61–68°F). For warmer rooms (20–24°C), choose our 1.0 TOG. For cooler environments or winter nights, our 3.5 TOG is ideal. The most important rule: never add extra layers on top of the sleep sack.

How do I choose the right size? ▾

Size by your baby's current weight, not age. The sleep sack should be roomy enough to allow leg movement but not so large that baby can slip down inside. Use our size chart above — when in doubt, size up for growing room.

Is this suitable from birth? ▾

Yes — our 0–6M size is suitable from birth (minimum 7lbs / 3.2kg). We recommend pairing with a swaddle for the first 0–8 weeks, then transitioning to the sleep sack once they start showing startle reflexes.

How do I wash it? ▾

Machine wash at 30°C on a gentle cycle with similar colours. Tumble dry on low or air dry flat. Do not bleach or iron. The zip can be protected during washing by zipping it closed before putting it in the machine.

We offer a 30-day no-questions-asked return policy. If you're not happy for any reason — wrong size, changed your mind, baby outgrew it already — contact us and we'll arrange a free return label.
