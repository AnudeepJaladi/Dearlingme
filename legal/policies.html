Refund Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

We want you and your little one to love every Dearling.me purchase. If something isn't right, we're here to help.

1. Returns

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery, provided the item is:

  • Unused and in its original condition
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Not a hygiene-sensitive item (see exceptions below)

To start a return, email info@dearlingme.com with your order number and reason for return. We will respond within 2 business days with return instructions.

2. Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned unless defective:

  • Feeding items (bottles, sippy cups, spoons) that have been used
  • Bath products that have been opened or used
  • Items marked as final sale

3. Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you by email. If approved, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item, defective product).

4. Damaged or Defective Items

If you received a damaged or defective item, please email info@dearlingme.com within 7 days of delivery with:

  • Your order number
  • A photo of the damage or defect
  • A brief description of the issue

We will send a replacement or issue a full refund at no cost to you — no need to return the damaged item.

5. Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong item, contact us within 7 days. We will ship the correct item at no additional charge and cover return shipping for the incorrect item.

6. Lost or Stolen Packages

If your tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your package, please:

  1. Check with neighbours and your building's mail area
  2. Wait 2 business days (occasionally packages are marked delivered early)
  3. Contact us at info@dearlingme.com — we will open an investigation with the carrier

We will offer a replacement or refund for packages confirmed lost by the carrier.

7. Return Shipping Costs

  • Change of mind returns: customer covers return shipping
  • Defective / wrong item: we cover return shipping

8. Exchanges

We do not process direct exchanges. If you'd like a different item, return your original purchase for a refund and place a new order.

9. Contact

For any return or refund queries, email info@dearlingme.com — we aim to respond within 24 hours.
